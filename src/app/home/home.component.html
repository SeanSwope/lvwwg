Welcome to the Lehigh Valley Woodworker's Guild. We hope you enjoy your visit. Please take some time to explore our site, read about us, and send us your comments. Located in eastern Pennsylvania, about 60 miles north of Philadelphia, our guild is made up of professional and amateur woodworkers. If you are new to woodworking, a good place to start is by attending one of our meetings. We meet monthly at Woodcraft on Lehigh Street in Allentown. We welcome all woodworkers, regardless of what they build, their level of skill, style of work, or their profession. Our goals are to do a few things:
  • Educate the public about our Guild and encourage participation in a hobby/profession that is creative and gratifying.
  • Meet other guilds and woodworkers, hopefully making new friends along the way.
  • Advance the knowledge of our members, serve our community and have fun while we're doing it.
  • Serve our community by giving back to needy children.
We would like to express our deepest thanks to the following companies. Without their generosity, we would not be able to bring smiles to children's faces.
  • Bally Block Company
  • Woodcraft Store and Neil Brown
